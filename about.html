Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Mechanical Design Engineer based in Ankara, Turkey, with over 5 years of hands-on experience across defense, aerospace, and unmanned aviation sectors.

My work spans the full product lifecycle — from early-stage concept development and 3D CAD modeling through to manufacturing drawing release, supplier coordination, and qualification testing. I've served as both a design contributor and a Product Owner on critical flight systems, giving me a rare combination of deep technical skill and project-level ownership.

I've worked in AS9100D-certified environments and delivered systems compliant with MIL-STD-810G/H and other military/aerospace standards. My designs have gone through formal qualification campaigns — including Safety of Flight testing — not just been handed off to production.

Now, I'm expanding into freelance work, bringing that same rigor and reliability to clients who need precision mechanical engineering without the overhead of a large firm.
